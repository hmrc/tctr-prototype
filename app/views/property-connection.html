People who should fill out the form
We need people who occupy the property to complete this form. In a non-domestic setting, the occupier could be a business, a charity, or a not-for-profit organisation. You must also fill out the form if you have a lease, tenancy, or agreement to occupy and the property is vacant.

Trustees for owners or occupiers
Owners and occupiers can ask someone to complete the form on their behalf. This could be an employee of their business, for example.

Agents for owners or occupiers
This form can also be completed by a professional agent whom you have instructed. Some people find it useful to enlist the services of an agent if hey have complicated business arrangments.
