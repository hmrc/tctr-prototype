Why we need this information
The VOA uses the trading history of businesses to help with non-domestic property valuations.

If you’ve occupied your premises for 3 years or more, you’ll be asked for details from your last three years of completed accounts. You won’t be asked to provide details from the current financial year.

If you first occupied the property within the last 3 years, you’ll be asked for details from any full years completed accounts you have. You’ll also need to provide details from the current financial year.
