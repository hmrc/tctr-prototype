Send information about your premises to the Valuation Office Agency

You must send the Valuation Office Agency (VOA) information about your business property if they have asked you to. You must also let us know if you no longer own or rent the property.

The information you provide will be used by the local authority to help calculate business rates in your area.

If you do not send the information within 56 days of getting the letter the VOA sent to you, you may have to pay a penalty.

What you’ll need

  • the reference number from the letter VOA sent you
  • trading information for the last 3 financial years, or since you occupied the property
  • details of any franchises or lettings run from the property as a speparate business (if applicable)

If you do not own the property, you’ll also need:

  • your lease or agreement, including the current rent you pay
  • information about any recent changes to your rent or any rent-free periods
  • information about any changes you've made to the property that could affect its value
